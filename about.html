Our Story

We are a small team of creators dedicated to bringing unique, handcrafted goods to your home.

Our Mission

Unfinished Project was founded on a simple principle: to celebrate the art of craftsmanship. In a world of mass-produced goods, we want to provide an alternative that values human touch, quality materials, and sustainable practices. We partner with local artisans to create pieces that tell a story and stand the test of time.

The Artisans

Our team is a collective of passionate artists, designers, and woodworkers. Each person brings a unique skill set and a deep love for their craft. From the potter's wheel to the loom, every item is a testament to their dedication and talent. We believe in fair wages and ethical sourcing, ensuring that our products are not only beautiful but also made with integrity.

Sustainability

We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact. Our materials are responsibly sourced, and we prioritize natural, renewable resources. Our packaging is made from recycled materials, and we continuously seek new ways to reduce waste and promote a healthier planet. Thank you for supporting a business that cares about both quality and the environment.
